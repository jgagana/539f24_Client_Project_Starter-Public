Fall 2024 Important Dates

Deadlines/Important Dates for Full Term Classes at UMSI

See the Academic Calendar for additional important dates!

September 16: Add/Drop Deadline for Fall 2024 full-term courses

The final day to drop a course with full tuition remission and without receiving a “W” on your transcript. Any changes to your academic
schedule after this point must follow the Late Add/Drop procedure.

October 14- October 15: Fall Break

There are no classes during Fall Break. Most university offices are still open to assist students during this time.

November 11: Backpacking begins

At this time, you will be able to add courses to your backpack to prepare for Winter 2025 scheduling. You will also be able to see your enrollment appointment. To review how to backpack, please see the Degree Progress and Registration Tab.

November 18-November 20: Registration for graduate students begins

Registration for graduate students begins based on enrollment appointment, which is viewable in Wolverine Access. Be sure to follow
your MSI degree requirements when creating your course schedule.

November 27-Novemeber 29th: Thanksgiving Break

Courses pause and university offices close during Thanksgiving break. University business resumes on Monday, December 2nd.

December 9: Last day of full-term classes for Fall 2024

This is also the last day that students can submit late add/drop/swap requests for Fall 2024 full-term classes. Please note that other
colleges might abide by different late add/drop/swap dates. This deadline is only for courses offered from UMSI.

December 12: Teaching Evaluations open to students

Your feedback plays a very important role in improving teaching and curriculum in the School of Information. Rest assured that all
evaluations are anonymous, and instructors do not see their results until after final grades have been submitted. You can access your
evaluations at https://umich.bluera.com/umich/

December 15: Commencement

More information about the Winter commencement ceremony can be found LINKED HERE.
